Hexagon Truth

The hexagonal consciousness movement is a worldwide community of scientists, artists, and thinkers dedicated to raising awareness of the illustrious hexagon, nature's perfect shape. Our community encompasses many diverse points of view, brought together by our love of hexagons and a belief in the key role hexagonal principles must play in our shared human future.

Hexagon Truth and the Global Hexagonal Awareness Project stand at the vanguard of the hexagonal movement, spreading the good news of hexagonal consciousness via diverse, interdisciplinary media across a variety of modalities. We view the hexagon as nothing less than the telos of the world made manifest — an unsurpassed expression of the deep intelligibility of nature.
