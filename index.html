Hexagon Truth is an interdisciplinary digital art project by hexagonal artist Graham Steele in partnership with the Global Hexagonal Awareness Project.

This site is presently under construction as part of an attempt to disentangle my brands. For more details on GHAP and it's disparate social media presences, please visit the suspiciously-similar-looking Hexagon.link site, or our legacy content site at Hexnet.org. The latter mostly exists for historical archival reasons at this point.
