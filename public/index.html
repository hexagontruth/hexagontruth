Hexagon Truth is an interdisciplinary digital art project produced in partnership with the Global Hexagonal Awareness Project.

For additional art NFTs, check out our profiles on SuperRare, OpenSea, or Foundation. Also be sure to stop by our Voxels gallery, which features both tokenized and untokenized Hexagon Truth pieces as well as a curated collection of works by other hexagonal artists.
